0pen_5pace is a platform for artists. Founded in 2015 & Established in 2018, the group of people behind 0pen_5pace content, the organisation and network of Creators, give life to the movement.

0pen_5pace started as an organisation that hosts events for upcoming poets, musicians, dancers, visual artists and many more, giving them an opportunity to bring their work to a live audience.

Over time 0pen_5pace has expanded. Members range from those who work with the organisation to those who support the movement. 0pen_5pace works with the artists as well the community. The organisation exists to Promote and Market Art to new markets whilst developing the local art and entertainment scene.
